Aromatic Blueberry Pie

A refeshing drink that will keep you hydrated like Poseidon.

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce vodka
  • 1 ounce blue curacao
  • 4 ounces lemonade
  • Garnish: lemon wheel
  • Garnish: maraschino cherry

Steps

  1. Add the vodka, blue curacao and lemonade to a shaker with ice and shake until well-chilled.
  2. Strain into a hurricane glass over crushed ice.
  3. Garnish with a lemon wheel and maraschino cherry.
